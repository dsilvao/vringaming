What is VR and Why should you care?

The purpose of this website is to introduce you to Virtual Reality Technology, detailing how it works, what is currently on offer and what the potential future is. While VR has been around for a long time many people aren’t fully familiar with what it is and how it works. Virtual Reality can be defined as a medium comprised of interactive computer simulations that record a user’s movements and senses and replace feedback to their senses to give the feeling of a virtual world. With this definition it makes it clear why Virtual reality technology is being used heavily in gaming. Virtual Reality technology even now can offer gamers a more immersive experience than the traditional form of gaming can, and the technology is only getting better, with researching being done and advancements being made at a remarkable rate. It is quite possible that VR will be the future of gaming. Already all the big console companies offer VR headsets as an add on to their existing consoles. Nintendo, Microsoft and Sony all offer a VR headset while there are several available for PC gamers. Even people who just play small games on their phones have VR options. This is website will detail how VR works, what it’s future is and what is available at this moment.
